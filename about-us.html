About Us

We are a group of university students inspired by our transportation struggles to provide a service that benefits individuals as well as the greater humanity. Our service allows easy, inexpensive, and temporary bicycle lending and renting. On a larger scale, re:CYCLE is provides access to a healthier mode of transportation. Choosing bikes for transportation instead of gas-consuming vehicles reduces pollution that penetrates the ozone layer which protects our precious planet from the deathly hot rays of the Sun. Money is not the main objective for our company or website. Rather, our aim is to encourage biking for its health and environmental benefits while providing a service that increases and eases bike accessibility around the world.

Mission Statement

re:CYCLE is determined to make an everlasting impact on this planet by providing a safe, easy-to-use service that increases the number of bikes on the road and decreases the amount of pollution in the air.
